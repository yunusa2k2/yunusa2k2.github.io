Yunusa is the founder of NewraLab, an AI research and development startup based in Suzhou, China, focused on building intelligent systems for autonomous technologies. With a PhD in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems from Beihang University, he brings deep expertise in computer vision, generative models, and natural language processing — blending academic research with practical innovation. Yunusa has published in, and reviewed for, top-tier conferences such as ACL, COLING, ACCV, ICMV, and ICCV. He actively contributes to the global AI community through peer reviews and collaborative research. NewraLab is driven by his passion for advancing AI through rigorous research and translating breakthroughs into real-world solutions. Outside of work, Yunusa enjoys football and boxing — bringing the same energy and discipline to his startup journey.
